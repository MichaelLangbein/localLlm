The history of artificial intelligence began in ancient times with philosophers pondering the nature of thought
and reasoning. In the 20th century, Alan Turing proposed the Turing Test, a method to determine if a
machine can exhibit intelligent behavior.
The field exploded in the 1950s with the development of the first neural networks and expert systems.
Today, AI powers everything from recommendation algorithms to autonomous vehicles, revolutionizing industries
and raising ethical questions about machine consciousness and job displacement.
